Mac and Cheese

Description

This is my indulgent mac and cheese--the not counting calories, preemptive lactaid, creamy and cheesy, holiday mac and cheese. I serve this for special occasions, celebrations, and it is always the meal I bring to loved ones who need extra love.

  • 2 lb. Elbow Macaroni
  • 1.5 lb Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 lb Monterey Jack Cheese
  • 3 oz Parmesan Cheese
  • 8 oz Cream Cheese
  • 1/4 cup Flour
  • 1/2 lb Butter
  • 4 cups Heavy Cream/li>
  • 1 cup Whole Milk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Boil a large pot of water and cook the macaroni according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. Grate the cheddar, monterey jack, and parmesan cheese. Set aside.
  4. In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the flour and whisk until smooth. Cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Slowly whisk in the heavy cream and milk. Cook until the mixture is thick and coats the back of a spoon.
  6. Remove from heat and add the cream cheese. Stir until melted.
  7. Add the cheddar, monterey jack, and parmesan cheese. Stir until melted.
  8. Add the cooked macaroni and stir until well coated.
  9. Pour the mac and cheese into a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  10. Bake for 30 minutes or until bubbly and golden brown.
  11. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
  12. Enjoy!
