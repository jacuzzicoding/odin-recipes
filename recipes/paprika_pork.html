Paprika Pork

Description

These easy 15-minutes pan fried pork chops are served in a garlic paprika butter with fresh thyme, and are delicious served with mashed potatoes and peas.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless pork chops
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Steps For Success

  1. Season the pork chops with the smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, and salt.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add butter.
  3. Add the pork chops to the skillet and cook for 4-5 minutes per side, or until the pork reaches an internal temperature of 145Â°F.
  4. Remove the pork chops from the skillet and let rest for 3 minutes.
  5. Serve the pork chops with the garlic paprika butter and fresh thyme.
  6. Enjoy!
